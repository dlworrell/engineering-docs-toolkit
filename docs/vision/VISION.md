# EDT Vision

## Vision Statement

The Engineering Documents Toolkit (EDT) aims to be an open, standards-based semantic document engineering platform that preserves technical knowledge independently of presentation.

EDT is designed to ensure that engineering, scientific, legal, and technical publications remain understandable, verifiable, reproducible, and publishable across generations of software, hardware, and publication technologies.

## Long-Term Objectives

- Preserve semantic meaning rather than visual appearance.
- Adopt open standards wherever practical.
- Normalize documents into a stable canonical representation (EDOM).
- Support multiple import and publication formats from the same semantic source.
- Preserve provenance throughout the document lifecycle.
- Enable reproducible publishing and long-term archival.
- Support profile-driven document families without changing the core platform.

## Future Direction

EDT will continue to expand through standards integration, richer semantic models, additional importers and publishers, improved accessibility, profile-driven processing, and advanced engineering knowledge extraction.

The platform's success will be measured not by the number of supported file formats, but by its ability to preserve technical knowledge accurately and durably over time.
